Welcome to MSE 2034 Introduction to Materials Engineering

Materials are the foundation of our world, from natural fibers like wool to synthetic products like polyethylene. In this course, we explore the major classes of materials: metals, ceramics, and polymers. Together, we will explore a deeper understanding of how the properties of materials depend on their internal structure.

As materials engineers, we recognize that a material's structure—and consequently its properties—can be controlled through thoughtful design and processing. Conversely, ignoring the process history of a material will often result in material failure. We will explore several cases throughout this course.
