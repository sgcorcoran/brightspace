Learning Objectives

Chapter 1: Introduction to Materials

By the end of this chapter, students should be able to:

    Core Principles of MSE

  1. Explain the interrelationship between structure, properties, processing, and performance.
  2. Identify the different classes of materials.

    3. Relevance & Importance of MSE

  3. Highlight historical examples of MSE driving technological progress.
  4. Provide examples of how MSE has contributed to advancements in other engineering fields.

    5. Career Opportunities (BS degree)

  5. Identify the roles of MSE graduates in the workplace.
  6. Identify the various industries that employ MSE graduates.
  7. Idenfity the workplace environments in which MSE graduates are employed.

    8. Exciting Challenges & Future Directions

  8. Discuss the role MSE graduates play in shaping the future.
  9. Identify examples of current developments in MSE research.
  10. Discuss the opportunities for advanced degrees in MSE (MS, PhD)
