Learning Objectives

Chapter 1: Introduction to Materials

By the end of this chapter, students should be able to:

  1. Explain what is meant by engineered materials.
  2. Classify materials as either: metals, ceramics, or polymers.
  3. Explain why a broader classification scheme such as metals, ceramics, glasses, polymers, elastomers, and composites may be more practical.
  4. Recognize the classes of materials on a materials property chart.
  5. Using the terms structure, properties, processing explain underlying principle of the field of materials science and engineering.
