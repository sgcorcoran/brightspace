Properties: It all starts with the atom!

In this chapter, we will explore atomic structure—the building blocks of all materials. In the chapters that follow, we’ll expand our focus to how materials are structured at different length scales, from individual atoms to large-scale arrangements. You’ll discover how understanding these structures allows scientists and engineers to design materials with a wide range of tailored properties, from lightweight but strong metals to flexible plastics and even advanced materials used in cutting-edge technology.
