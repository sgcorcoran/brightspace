Worked Example: theoretical density

Calculate the theoretical density given the atomic weight, atomic radius, and crystal structure.

Objectives:

  1. Be able to calculate the theoretical density of a material knowing the element type and crystal structure.
  2. Be able to discuss / explain the theoretical density equation based on a unit analysis.
