Sean Corcoran MSE demonstrations

Site for testing new demonstrations for an introductory course in materials science and engineering.

planes_builder

Build the FCC and BCC structure and highlight the atoms on a given low index plane. The viewer allows for the removal of atoms so that individual planes can be viewed to show atom packing.

ABC packing

ABC packing (more funtionality to come). Currently just shows the three (111) type planes stacked for the FCC structure.

bcc_cut_spheres

Join multiple cut-sphere model BCC unit cells together. Helps to see the fraction of atoms within the unit cell.

fcc_unit_cell

FCC unit cell rotatable in space. Change the radius of the atoms with a slider to view an exploded-view or space-filled model.

Miller indices

Give a set of h,k,l plane indices and the plane will be plotted within a cubic unit cell.

Worked Example: Size of Interstitial Site

Walk through for the calculation of the size of the BCC tetrahedral site. The calculation only relys on the coordinates of the interstitial atom and closest neighbor.

Worked Example: Atomic Packing Factor

Step by step walk through for the derivation of the atomic packing factor for the BCC structure.
