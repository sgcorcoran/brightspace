Learning Objectives

Chapter 3: Structures of Materials

By the end of this chapter, students should be able to:

  1. Explain the difference between atomic structure, atomic arrangement, microstructure, and macrostructure.
  2. Identify the 14 Bravais lattices and 7 crystal systems.
  3. Perform crystal structure calculations.
  4. Determine unit cell properties of the FCC and BCC systems.
