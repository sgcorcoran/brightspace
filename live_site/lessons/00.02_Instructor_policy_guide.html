General Policies

Students with special needs or circumstances should feel free to meet during office hours or by appointment.

Honor Code:

All work, including homework, quizzes, exams, etc must be completed individually unless otherwise stated. Mutual collaboration on homework is encouraged, but copying or sharing solutions is a violation. Clarify any doubts about honor code situations with the instructor.

Late Homework: Accepted but may be penalized.

Missed Tests: Make-up exams will be allowed for reasonable circumstances with prior arrangements or proper documentation.

If you require accommodations, please provide the necessary documentation as early as possible.
